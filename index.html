I'm a Gis Analyst

Helwan university, Cairo

My name is Abdelfattah and I am an aspiring data analyst with an emphasis on spatial political data and Geographic Information Science (GIS). As a former political organizer, I have direct experience on both the client and consultant side of data-driven project management. This grants me unique insight into what clients need and how data minded decision making empowers client solutions. , I have experience working with many forms of spatial data, including but not limited to , government political boundary file, geocoded point data, and terrain datasets gathered from lidar. My academic work covers a broad range of applications from urban planning to forest ecology, both through traditional GIS software such as Arc GIS, QGIS
